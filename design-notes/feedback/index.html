Feedback Form Design

The feedback form has two purposes:

  • Understanding the host's experience
  • Assessing the coverage of events

One problem from earlier versions of the form that people who hosted multiple events were unable to distinguish between their events. On that basis, we recommend separating Host and Event feedback into two separate forms - the host form needs to be sent in once only, and the event sent in for each event.

The design of each form should be simple and clear to navigate, with field validation and minimising the fields that the user needs to submit.
